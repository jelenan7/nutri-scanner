About Nutri Scanner

Nutri Scanner was created to make everyday food choices simpler, clearer and healthier. Instead of struggling with tiny labels on food packaging, the app lets you scan a barcode or search for a food item and instantly view key nutrition information presented in a clean and easy-to-read format.

All product data is retrieved through the Open Food Facts API, a global open database of food products. Since ingredients and nutrition values can vary by country or brand, anyone with allergies or medical conditions should always verify the information on the original product packaging.

Nutri Scanner is also a helpful tool for people tracking their diet. Whether you are looking for a chocolate bar that fits your calorie goals or searching for products with lower sugar, more protein or fewer carbohydrates, the app helps you quickly find options that match your nutritional needs.
